Grand View Research published a report on Home Decor Market Size, Share, & Trends Analysis. They state that the global home decor market size was valued at 663.1 billion dollars in 2018. The report states: "The product demand is fueled by customer preferences for fancy items to decorate in homes." They go on to say: "The growth of the interior designing industry has paved the way for fancy and premium home décor products that provide a ravishing experience for the visitors. These fancy and premium products help in providing a spiritual and aesthetic pleasure, which also adds to the sophistication of consumers living in the house."

The report talks about mass produced items, such as wooden flooring and furniture, but the obvious ultimate "fancy and premium home décor products that provide a ravishing experience" is unique one-of-a-kind fine art.
